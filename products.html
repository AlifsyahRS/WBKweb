About WINCore

WINCore is an Integrated, Flexible, Open and Real-Time online Core Banking System that has better Control to support Bank’s Operation (Non-Forex Banks)

WINCore Banking System is designed and developed based on the following concept as the foundation across all modules:
  • Customer Relationship Management
  • Flexible (table parameter and extended)
  • User-friendly (menu and report user definable)
  • Open (easy to integrate to any 3rd Party System)
  • Scalable (can be upgraded along with the bank’s growth)
  • Comply and support to current BI regulations and Future (LBU Basel, LBBU, LHBU, LKPBU, SID, LLD, RTGS, SKN, KYC, DHN, PPATK (STR & CTR), PSAK 50/55)
  • Report Design Generator
  • Online & Real-time Processing (up to Accounting Level)
  • Better control and security (Authorization and Verification)



Running on the Leading edge IBM iSeries technology platform, it will make WINCore Banking System:
  • OPEN - easy to connect to any other system such as Tax-online, Host-to-host to Telco, PLN, ATM Switching and Shared ATM Network, etc.
  • SECURE - It's already proven that IBM iSeries has been very secure and no virus ever found so far
  • SCALABLE - it can be run from the smallest model of IBM iSeries up to the largest model. Banks can start using the right size of iSeries model and it can be upgraded easily when the bank grows. This means your investment is protected.


WINCore Product Family






Central Banking Module

Central Bank Report Module will allow the bank to build a standard Format Reports that needed by Central bank. This application will allow the bank to extract and integrating data from operational and external data into a single repository from which end users can easily make a Central Bank Reports, and send automatically to Central Bank.

The Central Bank Reporting Modules are:
  • LBU Basel
  • LBBU
  • LHBU
  • LKPBU


SIVES - The Signature

SIVES-The Signature is specially designed to address the signature verification system, as well as the other image such as company chop/stamp and photo. SIVES-The Signature is based on industry-standard technologies to be scalable, open and flexible, whether you are dealing with large or small number of signatures. Its secure server-based architecture offers you an electronic signature verification solution that fully meets your requirements. Therefore, considering the technology update and increasing demand of up-to-date signature or image/photo, SIVES-the Signature has taken these things into its development.

SIVES-The Signature has rich functionality from capturing the signature to reporting and even there are many unique features that will help the bank in operating and maintaining the signature. Key functionality that is specific created at SIVES-The Signature, mainly to focus on addressing the requirements and providing benefit to Operation, Security and Flexibility.
