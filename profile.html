About Us
PT. Warna Bintang Kreasi (WBK) is an IT solution provider which mainly focusing in providing solution to Banking Industry. Since it was set up in 2002, WBK has gained confidence from Banking Community and growing the market share.

Initially, WBK developed the so called Central Bank Reporting Modules that helps Banks in automating extracting data from Core Banking System and other sources and remapping into the format exactly as Central Bank Report requirement. There are more than 15 Banks are using our Central Bank Reporting Modules, both local and foreign Banks as well.

Besides that, our qualified consultant team have many experiences in implementing various Core Banking Solutions successfully in many Banks from small to large size Bank and simple to complex implementation. Bank Pembangunan Daerah Jawa Barat dan Banten (Bank Jabar Banten) is one of the success stories of our consultant team implementing Core Banking System and make the whole network of Bank Jabar Banten online.

2005
After successfully implementing other's Core Banking Solution, WBK decided to developed its own Core Banking Solution known as WINCore by Joint Application Development with BANK PRIMA MASTER which its head office in Surabaya.
